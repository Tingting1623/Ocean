Seafood Guide in Taiwan
Seafood Watch guides provide at-a-glance info on which fish is relatively abundant (okay to eat) and which species are overfished (need to avoid).

The principle of choosing seafood
1. Common species are better than rare fish.
2. Silver white fish is better than colorful fish.
3. MIgratory fish is better than sedentary fish.
4. Choose seafood that has traveled the least distance
5. Avoid longevity large predator fish.
6. Choose Farmed fish not wild fish.
7. Avoid farmed shrimp or salmon.
8. Choose seafood that is in the botton of the food chain.
